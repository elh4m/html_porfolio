The Best Movies According to Elham

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

a captivating journey into a mystical world, where a young girl embarks on a quest to save her parents and find her way home.

Lion King

a timeless Disney classic that tells the tale of Simba, a young lion prince, on his path to understanding the true meaning of responsibility and bravery.

The Wind rises

is a visually stunning and emotionally rich film by Hayao Miyazaki, exploring the life of Jiro Horikoshi, the man who designed Japanese fighter planes during World War II, blending historical narrative with a poignant love story.
